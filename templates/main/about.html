Our Mission: Make Learning Effective & Enjoyable

TestHub was founded in 2020 with a simple goal - to transform test preparation through technology and psychology.

Our Story

TestHub began as a university project between three computer science students who struggled with traditional study methods. Frustrated by passive learning techniques, we set out to create a platform that would make test preparation active, engaging, and data-driven.

What started as a simple quiz app has grown into a comprehensive learning platform used by thousands of students worldwide. Our team has expanded, but our commitment to effective learning remains unchanged.
